About Electron Builder

Electron Builder is a simple application that allows you to build Electron applications with ease; (and electron packager).



Fun facts:

The developer of Electron Builder is Evan Viele.

Evan Viele was only 12 when he first made Electron Builder

Electron Builder was built with Electron Builder! (via npm and electron)

The first version of Electron Builder, (Version: 1.0.0, Build: 1) was made in just 2 days.
