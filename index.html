Electron Builder Documentation/Getting Started

Electron Builder is designed to be simple. Electron Builder handles the hard work for you. Thats why the "Documentation" is more like a "Getting Started."

Step 1

Create your Electron app.

Step 2

Launch Electron Builder and configure your prefrences

Step 2.1 (Optional)

NOTE: Advanced options isnt working at the moment and will result with an error.

If you still want to add extra options, go to "View/Toggle Developer Tools," and go to the "Console" tab.
Press the "Build" button, and copy the command generated in the log. Paste that into your console and modify it. There you can run it.

Above the "Build" button, click "Show Advanced Options," and add additional flags if needed.

(Type your flags in as you would normally in electron-packager CLI, E.G. --app-version=1.0.0)

Step 3

Click "Build" and a log window will show up. You can close Electron Builder when the console window says "Complete. It is now safe to exit Electron Builder."

(It may include more data as well)

Thats it! Enjoy your app!
